Education and Training

AKP is dedicated to the betterment of digital society from grassroots. An informed digital society is a prosperous digital society, so education starts as early as primary and continues into professional life.

Description

Suitable for beginners to enter the world of the 21st century digital economy through fundamental concepts related to blockchain and cryptography. Covers important concepts that are required to fully appreciate what blockchain technology is, and subsequently for full appreciation of blockchain-enabled solutions and their vital role in regulated web3 infrastructures.

Learning Outcomes

Most students should be able to explain what trust is, why trust is the cornerstone of all economic activity, and how trust facilitates economic activity online and in real life; some students may be able to explain the importance of cryptography, game theory, and incentives in digital economies; the most erudite may be able to explain cryptography concepts.

Description

Suitable for learners seeking deeper theoretical knowledge on the infrastructures of the 21st century digital economy through advanced concepts related to blockchain and cryptography. Covers important concepts of blockchain technology is, which are required to fully appreciate blockchain-enabled solutions and their vital role in regulated web3 infrastructures.

Learning Outcomes

Most students should be able to explain why blockchain is important and what blockchain is; some students may be able to explain how blockchains work, including cryptographic concepts, and how blockchain is applied for different use cases; the most erudite may be able to discuss case studies of blockchain with respect to identity and privacy.

Description

Suitable for learners endeavouring to apply their new-found skills on the applications for the 21st century digital economy through a practical design project, presentation and symposium, and a practicum with industry professionals. Covers current events and trends from actual practitioners to facilitate the design and building of a regulated web3 application.

Learning Outcomes

Most students should be able to apply the ToIP or other Web3 models, GDPR and/or other privacy regulations, PCTF and/or other trust frameworks, etc. All students will be familiar with concepts in design thinking and project management. The most erudite students would be familiar with Ethereum, Hyperledger Fabric, Quorum, and R3 Corda.
