Pioneers of Web3 from Fundamental Building Blocks

Atomic Knowledge Proof (AKP) is named for the atomic processes of trust frameworks used to quantify trust. AKP is part of the global movement rebuilding the world wide web to facilitate human trust in the digital world through regulated application of blockchain and cryptography.

What do we do
Where we are from
The world wide web is undergoing a sea change. The dream: digital assets can be owned and transacted purely online. The challenges: privacy, security, and compliance. The blockchain space is no stranger to lofty dreams. The challenge is bridging dreams and reality.

TECHNICAL EXCELLENCE: AKP provides products and services to systems designers, integrators, and operators to launch and maintain applications for this new world wide web: Web3. The AKP software development kit gives any web application or ecosystem the capabilities expected of a regulated web3 solution. Components may be integrated by module or by functionality.

COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT: AKP also invests in training, coaching, and supporting students and startups with promising projects or learning potential. AKP sponsors and supports hackathons, contests, and international competitions. AKP develops and delivers education and training to students at the primary, secondary, and tertiary levels.

  • Entered the 2019 R3 Global Pitch Competition and placed in top 12 finalists, proposing the use of distributed ledger technology with zero-knowledge proof for privacy-preserving aviation maintenance records.

  • Entered into the 2019 International Blockchain Olympiad (IBCOL 2019) as a Greater Bay Area team (Guangzhou Civil Aviation College, Hong Kong Baptist University) and placed in 3rd overall (bronze medalist).

  • Received technical and business development support from the Hong Kong Blockchain Society (HKBCS) for prototyping.

  • Entered SIA AppChallenge and finished in 2nd round.

  • Incorporated in 2019 as a limited company in Hongkong SAR, China.

  • Accepted into 2019 Global Accelerator Academy cohort for Smart Airways, mentored by Cathay Pacific and demonstrated a proof-of-concept to its executives.

  • Accepted into 2020 pre-incubation STEP programme from the Hong Kong Science and Technology Park (HKSTP) and successfully graduated.

  • Expanded scope of offerings beyond the aerospace industry; company rebranded as Atomic Knowledge Proof (AKP).

  • Mentored teams from Canada, Mainland China, Hong Kong, Macau, Singapore, and Taiwan for the 2020 International Blockchain Olympiad (IBCOL 2020), sponsored by HSBC.

  • Mentored teams worldwide for the 2021 International Blockchain Olympiad (IBCOL 2021), sponsored by the government of Bangladesh.

  • Selected as a delivery partner for the 2022 Greater Bay Area Blockchain Olympiad programme, sponsored by the government of Hong Kong.

  • Accepted in 2021 into the IncuTech incubation programme of the Hong Kong Science and Technology Park (HKSTP).

  • Since 2022, AKP has launched pilots in the following industries: financial services, architecture/engineering/construction, property management, education and human resources, and healthcare.
